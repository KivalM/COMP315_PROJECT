How to play

Deception's Echo is a mystery quiz game.
You will be given clues in the conversation with the suspects. These range from the suspects' alibis to their motives. You will have to use these clues to find out who the culprit is.
Every round wil have a set of questions that you will have to answer. These questions will be based on clues given in the dialogue. You will have to answer these questions correctly.
The department has its eye on you. You will have to answer all the questions correctly to prove your worth.
Too many wrong answers and you will be fired.
