Deception's Echo: A Detective's Tale

How to play

Every character will have a question pool. Every question answer pair will include some information on either the murder or how to communicate with a character. For each stage you are permitted a limited number of questions you can ask. Ask the question in the wrong tone, or a question that already had an answer given to you, or a question that seems irrelevant to the mystery to your superiors, or questions that provoke a hostile response will show the department you are incapable. You will also be given an option to point out a contradiction in something a character says, this option will be used as a lifeline and will either increase the bar significantly or decrease it, it is the only thing that can decrease the bar.
